Company

Jukely

Role

Product Designer

Project

The first version of the “Refer a Friend” feature on Jukely for iOS was pretty bare-bones. Existing users could “Refer a Friend” and get $10 off their next month of service. In turn, the receiver of that referral would get $10 off their first month upon sign up. Over time, we saw that on average, less than 15% of new users came through these referrals.

At the time, this was the only metric by which we were evaluating the effectiveness and the impact of this feature. During a series of sprints focusing on improving user growth, I was tasked with rethinking the “Refer a Friend” feature on iOS, introducing it on Android and increasing the number of users coming in through referrals across the board.

Team

Tess (PM), Dan (ENG)

Customer Goals + Business KPI

Increase user referrals, Increase account sign ups, Decrease churn of refered users
