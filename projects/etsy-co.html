Custom Orders

Company

Etsy

Role

Product Designer

Project

Increasingly, Etsy sellers around the world are running their creative businesses on the go and making unique goods that are customized, personalized and created through collaboration with buyers.

Historically, designs, transaction details, and the buyer-seller relationship would evolve out of conversation across mobile and desktop devices, but the functionality to manage, track, and fulfill requests throughout the seller experience was unintuitive on web and nonexistent on Native. Over time, this hurt custom order conversion rates, seller app DAU and seller NPS.

In the Fall and Winter of 2017, I designed the Custom Orders experience on the buyer and seller facing Native apps.
