Vine Remix

Company

Vine/Twitter

Role

Lead Product Designer

Project

Vine Remix was a feature released to a select group of users that allowed Viners to easily cut, combine and edit audio and video sources from their library, from Twitter, and from trending Vines and videos to create new Vines.

Vines made using Vine Remix maintained a reference to each piece of source material accessible from the post itself, preserving attribution for creators. This enabled watchers to explore and discover trends and memes and then made it easy to make one of their own.

By surfacing memes and trends on Vine in a new and exciting way, Vine Remix broke down some of the barriers to creation, providing easy inspiration for users to create their own Vines and participate in ongoing cultural conversation.
