Hello!

I’m Jordan Obi and I’m a Nigerian-American digital product designer based in Brooklyn, NY.

In the past, I’ve worked on fun (🌟!) and culturally impactful products with thoughtful people at Vine, Twitter and Jukely. Today, I work on communication tools for creative entrepreneurs at Etsy.

I think a lot about Memetics, black culture, the characteristics of games, and the mechanics of social products. I love to detangle complex systems with gnarly edge-cases, champion ethics and the importance of craft, and be a full-hearted cheerleader for my friends and teammates ☺︎.

Feel free to reach out and say hi, ya'hear! 👋🏾
